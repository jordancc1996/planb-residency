Advisory board

Independent practitioners and former regulators who review our methodology, flag jurisdictional changes, and help us keep advisor-facing materials precise.

Governance

The board convenes quarterly to stress-test methodology changes, review how new statutes affect program comparisons, and approve disclosure language that accompanies broker introductions. Members serve in a personal capacity; Plan B Residency retains editorial independence and does not compensate board members for program-specific outcomes.

Composition

Roster slots include a former broker-dealer CCO with cross-border referral experience, an immigration economist with peer-reviewed publication history, and tax faculty or practice leaders with dual-licensure context (U.S. plus EU or Commonwealth). Full curricula vitae and conflict statements are furnished to enterprise subscribers under confidentiality for operational due diligence.

Inquiries

Media, academic, and counterparty diligence requests route through Advisor Relations with subject line “Board diligence.”
