European residency

Golden Visas

Residency-by-investment programs in the EU and EEA that confer legal residence, path-dependent permanence, and—in many cases—Schengen mobility. Capital may be deployed into regulated funds, qualifying real estate, or operating businesses depending on the jurisdiction.

Profiles separate “right to reside” from tax residence and tie minimum stays to statutory language so you do not over-promise mobility relative to clients’ Substantial Presence or treaty positions.
