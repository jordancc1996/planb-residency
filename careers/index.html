Careers

We recruit professionals who can read primary legal instruments, reconcile them with marketing claims from program promoters, and write for an audience that files Form ADV and CRS. Fluency in regulatory English is mandatory; additional working languages (e.g., Portuguese, Greek, Spanish) materially help given source-material review.

Open requisitions

When a role is live, it is listed here with reporting line, location or remote policy, and compensation band. Between postings we still review profiles that demonstrate securities or immigration regulatory experience combined with clear writing samples.

How to apply

Send CV, cover memo, and two writing samples (redacted client work acceptable) via Contact. Reference “Careers” in the first line of the message field.

LinkedIn

Follow firm updates on LinkedIn for new requisitions and methodology releases.
